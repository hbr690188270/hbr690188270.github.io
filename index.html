Bio

Bairu Hou is a 4th-year undergraduate student of School of Economics and Management, , Tsinghua University. He is going to join UCSB and work with Prof. Shiyu Chang. His research interest mainly lies in natural language processing and speech recognition.

Currently, he focuses on adversarial robustness in natural language processing. He also has some experiences on natural logic and semantics.

He has been a research assistant in THUNLP Group supervised by Prof. Zhiyuan Liu since 2018, work closely with Fanchao Qi and Yuan Zang.
